About Us

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize law enforcement strategies by providing an innovative interface that bridges FIR (First Information Report) data with advanced mapping techniques. Our platform generates crime hotspots based on crime types, employing color mapping to depict varying crime intensities, considering parameters like date/time. Additionally, we incorporate behavioral analysis to study criminal behavioral patterns, aiding law enforcement in crime prevention and strategizing crime intervention measures.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals with expertise in data analysis, software development, and law enforcement. We are committed to leveraging technology for the betterment of society and enhancing public safety.
